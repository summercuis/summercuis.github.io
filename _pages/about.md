Researcher in philosophy of education. I am a PhD candidate in the [Faculty of Education](https://education.unimelb.edu.au) at the University of Melbourne. My research examines how Chinese international students engage with generative AI in Australian higher education, drawing on a Heidegger–Dewey philosophical framework. I also serve on the committee of the [Philosophy of Education Society of Australasia (PESA)](https://www.pesa.edu.au).
